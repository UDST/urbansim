Core Models¶

Introduction¶

Most of UrbanSim’s models are implemented as specialized classes that help link pandas data structures with other operations. The model selections include:

  • Statistical Models
    • A regression model for predicting numeric data and a location choice model for matching choosers to their likely selections.
  • Supply and Demand Accounting
    • Adjust real estate prices to account for suppy and demand effects.
  • Transition and Relocation Models
    • For adding/removing members from a population and choosing movers.
